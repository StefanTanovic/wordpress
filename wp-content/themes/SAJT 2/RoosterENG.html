Welcome to my webpage.
To my clients I offer services from graphic and web design.

  • Web design represents graphic modeling of internet content with the help of graphic elements and programming languages. Web design encompasses many different skills, standards and disciplines used in the production and maintenance of websites. Web presentations are nowadays one of the most spread ways for advertising different products. The main purpose of website is to offer more information about products to potential client. Web designers are expected to fulfill necessary conditions of site considering its visual identity and functionality.

  • The design of the package is one of its most important characteristics, and often the most important one. It has become more important than the product itself. It sets a price of some products and represents the key element of marketing campaign (at the advertisement the package of specific appearance has the central place). Rooster studio offers the manufacture of the design for paper packages (different kinds of boxes, printed labels, etc…), so as the design of package for serials of products.

  • Logo or trade mark represents the group of graphic elements (typography, color, line…) arranged in a unique way. The main aim is for logo to be recognizable, visible, to differ from others, by its quality first. That is the way for products bearing those logos to become easily recognizable. Logo is a kind of brand of a company or institution. Trademarks have become one of the basic ways of visual communication.

  • As a design discipline, graphic design has as an assignment graphic design of communication of different media. It encompasses the whole design of trademark, logotype and promo material (business cards, flyers, memos, envelopes, paper bags, folders…). With the development of press it has become possible to transfer adverts to other materials, so marketing on pens, lighters, cars in order to promote them, has been done in that way.
